Intresting facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons actually change their colour due to a number of different factors, including their mood, changes in light or temperature, or the humidity of their environment.
  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons take a while to hatch, and don’t live very long

Intresting facts about Pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.
  • Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo.

Intresting facts about Pigeons

  • They understand space and time.
  • They can find their nest from upto 1300 miles away.
  • Some pigeons actually distract falcons with white rump feathers.

Intresting facts about Camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
